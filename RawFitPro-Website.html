The Origin Story

Started With Nothing.
Built Everything.

"No gym. No weights. No money. Just the ground under my feet and the fire to make it work."

Before the certifications, before the client roster, before the platform — there was Raw Human Fitness. Joshua started training people outdoors because that was what was available. Parking lots. Open fields. Public parks. He flipped tires, had clients carry logs, built obstacle courses out of whatever he could find for free.

The results were undeniable. Clients got stronger, leaner, and tougher — not despite the raw conditions, but because of them. No machine to make it easy. No padding to soften the fall. Just raw effort producing real results.

That philosophy never changed. The equipment got better. The platform got professional. But the standard stayed exactly the same: show up, work hard, no excuses.

Day 1
Raw Human Fitness born. First clients trained in open fields — tires, logs, and obstacle courses built from nothing.
Year 2
Word spread. Results spoke louder than any gym membership could. Client roster grew purely by referral.
Year 5
Certified and expanding. Professional credentials added, indoor and outdoor programming merged.
Now
RawFitPro. Full professional platform, 5 Northshore locations, online coaching worldwide. Raw roots intact.
